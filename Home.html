WELCOME TO THAKUR COLLEGE OF SCIENCE & COMMERCE



Speciality

Our college has accomplished a spectacular growth over the last two decades of its journey towards excellence in Education and emerged as a leading Higher Educational Institution in Mumbai. The present total strength of our Degree College [Science & Commerce] is 7587 and the Junior Section is of 5867 students.




Sports

The College Gymkhana facilitates the needs of students who play Indoor games like Martial Arts, Judo, Carrom, Chess, Boxing, etc. whereas a Large stadium with viewing gallery and other infrastructural facilities provide an ideal ambience for outdoor games like Cricket, Football, Volleyball, Throwball, Kabaddi, Basketball, etc.




Festival

As an institution, we are known for our magnanimous fests: Taarangan and Trishna. Apart from annual fests, different departments conduct different events. The Biotechnology department conducts four events: Biochroma, Eureka, Mindscape Fusion. Sanjeevni is conducted by the Department of Botany.
